Genesis 5
This is the book of the generations of Adam When God created man he made him in the likeness of God
Male and female he created them and he blessed them and named them Man when they were created
When Adam had lived years he fathered a son in his own likeness after his image and named him Seth
The days of Adam after he fathered Seth were years and he had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days that Adam lived were years and he died
When Seth had lived years he fathered Enosh
Seth lived after he fathered Enosh years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Seth were years and he died
When Enosh had lived years he fathered Kenan
Enosh lived after he fathered Kenan years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Enosh were years and he died
When Kenan had lived years he fathered Mahalalel
Kenan lived after he fathered Mahalalel years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Kenan were years and he died
When Mahalalel had lived years he fathered Jared
Mahalalel lived after he fathered Jared years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Mahalalel were years and he died
When Jared had lived years he fathered Enoch
Jared lived after he fathered Enoch years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Jared were years and he died
When Enoch had lived years he fathered Methuselah
Enoch walked with God after he fathered Methuselah years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Enoch were years
Enoch walked with God and he was not for God took him
When Methuselah had lived years he fathered Lamech
Methuselah lived after he fathered Lamech years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Methuselah were years and he died
When Lamech had lived years he fathered a son
and called his name Noah saying Out of the ground that the LORD has cursed this one shall bring us relief from our work and from the painful toil of our hands
Lamech lived after he fathered Noah years and had other sons and daughters
Thus all the days of Lamech were years and he died
After Noah was years old Noah fathered Shem Ham and Japheth
